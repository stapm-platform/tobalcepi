# Synergistic effects of tobacco and alcohol risks

Synergistic effects of tobacco and alcohol risks

## Usage

``` r
tob_alc_risk_int
```

## Format

A data table. We currently include estimates of synergistic effects for
oral, pharyngeal, laryngeal and oesophageal cancers (we describe these
data and their sources in our tobacco risk functions report Webster et
al. (2018) ). The data sources we use are Prabhu et al. (2014) and
Hashibe et al. (2009) .
