# Names of tobacco-related diseases

Names of tobacco-related diseases

## Usage

``` r
tob_disease_names
```

## Format

A character vector containing the names of the 52 disease categories of
smoking related diseases Webster et al. (2018) .

## References

Webster L, Angus C, Brennan A, Gillespie D (2018). “Smoking and the
risks of adult diseases.”
[doi:10.15131/shef.data.7411451](https://doi.org/10.15131/shef.data.7411451)
.
